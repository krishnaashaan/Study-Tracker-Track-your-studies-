Your Cosmic Learning Journey

Welcome to your Study Galaxy! This visualization represents your learning journey as a cosmic galaxy. Each star is a subject you've studied, with orbiting planets representing specific topics. The size and brightness of stars reflect your study time, while connections between stars show related subjects.

Stars are categorized using the OBAFGKM spectral classification system, from hot blue giants (most study time) to cool red dwarfs (less study time). The more you study a subject and maintain streaks, the more massive and hotter your star becomes!

Explore your galaxy by dragging to move around and using the mouse wheel to zoom. Click on any star to see detailed information about that subject.
